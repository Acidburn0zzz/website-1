Welcome!

You've reached the site for the Arch Linux Women project. Arch Linux Women (Arch Women or A-W for short) is an all inclusize organization of Arch Linux enthusiasts with a focus on helping more women become involved in the Arch Linux community and FOSS. To do this, we provide a safe space for people of all genders and backgrounds to share skills, learn, network and socialize. Anyone with an interest in our mission is welcome to participate.
